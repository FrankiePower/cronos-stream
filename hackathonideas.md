Based on Austin Griffith's thread from January 13, 2026 (the Ethereum/Base-focused x402 Hackathon), he highlighted his personal "standout winning projects" as the top submissions. #### Additional Projects (Honorable Mentions & Other Submissions)
These were mentioned in thread replies or surfaced in related X posts as hackathon submissions (not explicitly "winners" per Austin, but part of the event). Some may overlap with Solana's separate x402 hackathon, but I've focused on Ethereum-relevant ones:

- **RaidGuild Submissions** (@RaidGuild / @DekanBro): Three team entries including a one-click deploy facilitator (self-host or $5/month hosted), and an x402 donate app. Demo dashboard: [cohort10-next-x402-dashboard-psi.vercel.app](https://cohort10-next-x402-dashboard-psi.vercel.app/).
- **Oops402Pay** (@oops402pay / mentioned by @alerios): Client-side project for handling x402 payments/refunds.
- **AgentARC** (@aswinrajkailath / @_buildbear): Open-source security layer for on-chain AI agents; validates intents, simulates executions, and blocks unsafe txns before signing. [Post](https://x.com/aswinrajkailath/status/2008429865402609731).
- **Slophouse Cinema** (@CrackedUnc): Video-based submission (details sparse, appears to be a creative x402 integration). [Demo Video](https://x.com/CrackedUnc/status/2008422122742219262).
- **ComposeMarket** (@compose_market): Drag-and-drop builder for composing agentic workflows as nested NFTs with x402-powered payable APIs. [Demo File](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1PbsNVeoMr-Zw1PCeZ7F6kc9AbqT1orl6/view?usp=sharing).
- **Agentokratia** (@agentokratia / @PIsajeski): Zero-code x402 paywall for agents (nginx config only); includes session-based payments, on-chain reviews (ERC-8004), no fees. Live on Base Sepolia: [agentokratia.com](https://agentokratia.com). [Demo Video](https://x.com/PIsajeski/status/2008264891187294617). (Extension of x402-escrow.)
- **TermiX Intelligence Marketplace** (@termix_ai): Agent-native marketplace where trading agents pay for insights via x402 (pay-per-result, confidence-based). [Demo Video](https://x.com/termix_ai/status/2008032864693293379).
- **x402MKT Token Launchpad** (@maxbuidl): Presale launchpad using x402 for contributions; refunds if goal unmet, tied to secure wallets. [Demo Video](https://x.com/maxbuidl/status/2008026934853124292).
- **Simmer** (@TheSpartanLabs): AI-native prediction markets; agents trade autonomously, pay for intelligence via x402 (low-cost runs, e.g., $0.1 for 10 trades). [Demo Video](https://x.com/TheSpartanLabs/status/2008008432151097570).
- **Autoincentive** (@Autoincentiv3): System for AI agents and x402 to build business momentum (details vague in post).
- **Verifiable Green Compute Network** (@0xMGLV): Renewable-powered GPU routing for AI jobs; verifiable execution, Chainlink integration, on-chain payouts. [Demo](https://lektra-labs.github.io/verifiable-compute-network/). [Demo Video](https://x.com/0xMGLV/status/2005650534985068586).

These projects collectively advance x402 by tackling gas costs, trust, scalability, and agent economies.
